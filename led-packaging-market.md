<p><strong>The LED Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD Packaging</li><li>COB Packaging</li><li>CSP Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LED packaging market comprises three main types: SMD packaging, COB packaging, and CSP packaging. SMD packaging is the most common type, with individual LEDs mounted on a surface. COB packaging involves multiple LED chips mounted directly on a substrate, reducing thermal resistance. CSP packaging is the latest technology, with a smaller footprint and higher efficiency due to a shorter distance between the LED chip and the lens. Each type of packaging offers unique benefits for different applications in the LED industry.</p></p>
