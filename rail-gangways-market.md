<p><ul><li>Single Piece Rail Gangways</li><li>Two Piece Rail Gangways</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single Piece Rail Gangways are fixed rail gangways that are typically made of a single piece of material and are permanently attached to the train or platform. They are commonly used in locations where space is limited or where a fixed gangway is preferred. </p><p>Two Piece Rail Gangways consist of two separate sections that can be connected and disconnected as needed. These gangways offer more flexibility in terms of length and positioning, making them suitable for trains of varying sizes or configurations. They are often used in situations where adjustable gangways are required for different types of trains.</p></p>
