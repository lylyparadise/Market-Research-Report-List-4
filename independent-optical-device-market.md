<p><ul><li>Active Device</li><li>Passive Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The independent optical device market consists of two main types: active devices and passive devices. Active devices, such as lasers and optical amplifiers, require an external power source to function and actively manipulate the signal passing through them. On the other hand, passive devices, like splitters and filters, do not require external power and simply transmit, split, or filter the incoming signal. Both active and passive devices play a crucial role in the optical communication network industry, providing essential functionalities for transmitting and processing optical signals.</p></p>
