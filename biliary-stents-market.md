<p><p>Biliary stents are devices used to relieve obstructions in the bile duct. There are two main types of biliary stents: metal stents and plastic stents. Metal stents are made of stainless steel or nitinol and provide excellent long-term patency, while plastic stents are typically made of polyethylene or silicone and are more commonly used in short-term cases. The choice between metal and plastic stents depends on factors such as the location and nature of the obstruction, patient's condition, and the desired duration of stent placement.</p></p>
