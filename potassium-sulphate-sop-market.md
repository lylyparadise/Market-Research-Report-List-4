<p><p>Potassium Sulphate (SOP) is available in two main market types - Powder SOP and Granular SOP. Powder SOP is finely ground and easily soluble in water, making it suitable for foliar spraying or fertigation. Granular SOP is formed by compacting powder SOP into granules, providing slow-release nutrients for crops. Both forms of SOP are used as fertilizers to provide potassium and sulfur to plants, helping to improve yield and quality of crops.</p></p>
