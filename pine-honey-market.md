<p><p>Pine honey is available in three main market types: extracted honey, pressed honey, and comb honey. Extracted honey is produced by extracting honey from the honeycomb using centrifugal force or gravity. Pressed honey is obtained by pressing honeycomb cells to release the honey. Comb honey is sold in its original beeswax comb, providing a unique and natural way to enjoy honey. Each market type offers a different experience for consumers seeking the distinct taste and texture of pine honey.</p></p>
