<p><p>Anesthesia disposables market includes various types such as LMAs (laryngeal mask airways), breathing circuits, ETTs (endotracheal tubes), resuscitators, and anesthesia face masks. LMAs are used to maintain an airway during surgery, while breathing circuits deliver anesthetic gases to the patient. ETTs are inserted into the trachea to ensure proper ventilation. Resuscitators are used to provide manual ventilation in emergency situations. Anesthesia face masks are used to administer anesthesia gases and oxygen to patients during surgery.</p></p>
