<p><strong>The Real Time PCR Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-step Real Time PCR</li><li>Two-step Real Time PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Real Time PCR kits are used for quantitative analysis of DNA or RNA in real time. One-step Real Time PCR kits combine reverse transcription and PCR in a single tube, simplifying the process. Two-step Real Time PCR kits separate reverse transcription and PCR into two steps, allowing for better control over each process. Both types of kits are essential tools in molecular biology research, diagnostics, and forensic analysis due to their high sensitivity, specificity, and efficiency in detecting and quantifying nucleic acids.</p></p>
