<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Specialty Fuel Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Deposit Control Additives</li><li>Cetane Improvers</li><li>Antioxidants</li><li>Lubricity Improvers</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deposit control additives are used to prevent the buildup of deposits in fuel systems, improving engine performance. Cetane improvers increase the cetane number of diesel fuel, enhancing combustion efficiency. Antioxidants prevent the oxidation of fuel, extending its shelf life. Lubricity improvers enhance lubrication properties of fuel, reducing engine wear. Other specialty fuel additives include cold flow improvers, corrosion inhibitors, and dye markers. Each type of additive serves a specific purpose in improving the quality and efficiency of fuel for various applications.</p></p>
