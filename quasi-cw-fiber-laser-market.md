<p><p>A quasi CW (continuous wave) fiber laser is a type of laser that combines the benefits of both pulsed and continuous wave lasers. It emits laser light in short pulses with high peak power, similar to a pulsed laser, but with a longer average power output similar to a continuous wave laser. This makes quasi CW fiber lasers ideal for applications such as material processing, medical procedures, and research.</p><p>The Quasi CW Fiber Laser Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.1% during the forecast period.
