<p><strong>The Vascular Injury Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lateral Arteriorrhaphy/Venorrhaphy</li><li>Patch Angioplasty</li><li>Bypass Graft</li><li>Extraanatomic Bypass</li><li>Thrombectomy</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vascular injury treatment market includes various types such as lateral arteriorrhaphy/venorrhaphy, patch angioplasty, bypass graft, extraanatomic bypass, thrombectomy, and others. Lateral arteriorrhaphy/venorrhaphy involves suturing of the injured blood vessel. Patch angioplasty uses a patch to repair the vessel. Bypass graft involves creating a new pathway for blood flow. Extraanatomic bypass redirects blood flow through alternative routes. Thrombectomy removes blood clots from the vessel. Other treatments may include stenting or embolization depending on the severity of the injury.</p></p>
