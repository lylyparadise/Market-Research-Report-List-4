<p><p>Chemical foaming agents are used in plastics to create a cellular structure and reduce density, improving properties such as stiffness and thermal insulation. Endothermic foaming agents absorb heat during the foaming process, resulting in lower processing temperatures and energy savings. Compound foaming agents combine different chemicals to achieve specific foaming properties, such as improved cell structure and increased expansion ratio. Both types of foaming agents offer opportunities for manufacturers to optimize plastic processing and enhance product performance.</p></p>
