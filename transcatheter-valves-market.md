<p><p>The Transcatheter Valves Market consists of three main types: Transcatheter Aortic Valve, Transcatheter Pulmonary Valve, and Transcatheter Mitral Valve. These valves are minimally invasive alternatives to traditional open-heart surgery for patients with valvular heart disease. Transcatheter Aortic Valve is used to treat aortic valve stenosis, while Transcatheter Pulmonary Valve is used for treating pulmonary valve regurgitation or stenosis. Transcatheter Mitral Valve is utilized for patients suffering from mitral valve regurgitation or stenosis.
