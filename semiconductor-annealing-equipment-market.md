<p><strong>The Semiconductor Annealing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lamp-based</li><li>Laser-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor annealing equipment can be categorized into two main types - lamp-based and laser-based. Lamp-based annealing equipment uses intense light emitted from a lamp to heat the semiconductor material, while laser-based annealing equipment utilizes focused laser beams for heating. Both types of equipment are commonly used in the semiconductor industry for processes such as crystallization and activation of dopants. Lamp-based equipment is more widely used for larger area treatments, while laser-based equipment is preferred for precise and localized annealing.</p></p>
