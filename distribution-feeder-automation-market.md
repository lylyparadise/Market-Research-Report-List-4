<p><p>Distribution Feeder Automation market types include Fault Location, Isolation, Service Restoration, and Automatic Transfer Scheme. Fault Location helps to pinpoint the exact location of a fault in the feeder system for quick resolution. Isolation capabilities enable the system to isolate the faulted section, minimizing impact on the rest of the network. Service Restoration automatically restores power to unaffected areas after a fault has been cleared. Automatic Transfer Scheme ensures seamless transfer of power sources in case of outages, ensuring continuous service.</p></p>
