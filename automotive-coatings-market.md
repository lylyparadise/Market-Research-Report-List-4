<p><p>Automotive coatings are divided into four main types based on their applications. Body coatings are used to protect the exterior of the vehicle from corrosion and damage. Interior parts coatings are applied to enhance the durability and aesthetics of components inside the vehicle. Exterior parts coatings are designed to withstand harsh environmental conditions and maintain the appearance of external components. Refinish coatings are used for repairing and repainting vehicles to restore their original look and protect them from further wear and tear.</p></p>
