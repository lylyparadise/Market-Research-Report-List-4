<p><p>Surface concrete vibrators are categorized into three types based on their frequency of vibration- High Frequency, Medium Frequency, and Low Frequency. High Frequency vibrators operate at a rate greater than 10,000 vibrations per minute and are suitable for thin concrete layers. Medium Frequency vibrators operate between 5,000 to 10,000 vibrations per minute and are used for medium depth concrete placements. Low Frequency vibrators operate at a rate less than 5,000 vibrations per minute and are designed for thick concrete layers or special applications.</p></p>
