<p><p>Highly-elastic couplings are categorized into two main types: Spiral Groove Type and Parallel Groove Type. Spiral Groove Type couplings are designed with spiral grooves to provide flexibility and allow for angular misalignment. Meanwhile, Parallel Groove Type couplings have parallel grooves that provide high torque transmission capacity and compensate for parallel misalignment. Both types are commonly used in various industries for connecting shafts and transmitting power efficiently while accommodating different types of misalignment.</p></p>
