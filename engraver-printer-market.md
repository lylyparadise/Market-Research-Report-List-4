<p><p>Engraver printers are classified into two main types based on their printing mechanism: flat printers and roller printers. Flat printers utilize a flat printing surface to create detailed engravings on various materials, while roller printers use a cylindrical roller to transfer prints onto surfaces. Both types of engraver printers are popular in the market for their precision and versatility in producing high-quality engravings on items such as jewelry, awards, and signage. Customers may choose between flat and roller printers based on their specific printing needs and preferences.</p></p>
