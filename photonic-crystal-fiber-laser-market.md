<p><strong>The Photonic Crystal Fiber Laser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photonic crystal fiber laser market can be categorized into two types: Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser and Pulsed Fiber Laser. Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser operates continuously, emitting a constant laser beam, making it suitable for applications requiring a steady output. On the other hand, Pulsed Fiber Laser produces periodic bursts of laser energy, making it ideal for applications such as material processing and medical procedures that require precise control and high peak power. Both types of fiber lasers cater to different requirements in diverse industries.</p></p>
