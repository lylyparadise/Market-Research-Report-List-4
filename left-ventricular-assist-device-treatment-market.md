<p><p>Left ventricular assist device (LVAD) treatment market includes various types of therapies such as destination therapy, bridge to transplantation, bridge to recovery, and bridge to destination. Destination therapy involves long-term support for patients who are not eligible for heart transplants. Bridge to transplantation is a temporary solution until a suitable donor heart is found. Bridge to recovery supports patients while their heart recovers. Bridge to destination therapy is for patients who are not candidates for transplantation but may receive the device as a permanent solution.</p></p>
