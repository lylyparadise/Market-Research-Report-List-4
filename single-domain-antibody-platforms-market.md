<p><p>Single domain antibody platforms have various market types including multi-specific, bispecific, multivalent, and mono-specific antibodies. Multivalent single-domain antibodies have multiple binding sites, enhancing their binding affinity and efficacy. Mono-specific single-domain antibodies target a single antigen, providing a focused approach to therapy.</p></p>
