<p><p>Touch screens in mobile devices are available in various market types, including capacitive touch screens, resistive touch screens, surface acoustic wave touch screens, and infrared touch screens. Capacitive touch screens are most commonly used due to their sensitivity, durability, and multi-touch capabilities. Resistive touch screens are cost-effective and resist scratches but are less sensitive. Surface acoustic wave touch screens offer high image clarity and durability. Infrared touch screens rely on infrared light to detect touch, providing high accuracy and durability.</p></p>
