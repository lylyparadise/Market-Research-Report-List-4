<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Frozen Dough Improver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Universal Type</li><li>Special Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Frozen dough improvers can be classified into two main types: Universal Type and Special Type. Universal Type improvers are designed to work well with a variety of dough types, providing general benefits such as increased volume, improved texture, and extended shelf life. On the other hand, Special Type improvers are formulated for specific types of dough, offering targeted solutions for challenges like poor fermentation, weak gluten development, or excessive enzyme activity. Both types cater to the diverse needs of bakeries and food manufacturers, ensuring optimal dough performance and product quality.</p></p>
