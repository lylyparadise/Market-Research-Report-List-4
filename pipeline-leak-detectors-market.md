<p><p>Pipeline leak detectors are essential in preventing environmental disasters and ensuring the safety of pipelines. There are different types of leak detectors available in the market, including pressure measurement and flow measurement systems. Pressure measurement detectors assess any changes in pressure within the pipeline, indicating a potential leak. Flow measurement detectors monitor the flow of the substance within the pipeline and can detect any discrepancies that may signal a leak. Both types of detectors are crucial in maintaining the integrity of pipelines and preventing leakage incidents.</p></p>
