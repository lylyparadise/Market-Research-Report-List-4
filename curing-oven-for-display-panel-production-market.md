<p><p>Curing ovens for display panel production come in two main types: hot air heating and infrared (IR) heating. Hot air heating ovens use a convection system to circulate heated air around the panels to cure the coatings. On the other hand, IR heating ovens use infrared radiation to directly heat the panels and cure the coatings. Both types of ovens have their own advantages and are commonly used in the production of display panels for various electronic devices.</p></p>
