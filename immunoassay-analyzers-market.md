<p><p>Immunoassay Analyzers market offers various types of testing methods including Immunofluorescence, Chemiluminescence, ELISA, and Radioimmunoassay. Immunofluorescence uses fluorescent dyes to detect target antigens, while Chemiluminescence measures light emitted during a chemical reaction. ELISA involves enzyme-labeled antibodies to detect specific proteins or antibodies in a sample. Radioimmunoassay uses radioactive tracers to measure the levels of antigens in a sample. Each method offers unique advantages and is commonly used in clinical diagnostics and research settings.</p></p>
