<p><strong>The Toasted Malted Wheat Flour Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diastatic Toasted Malted Wheat Flour</li><li>Non-Diastatic Toasted Malted Wheat Flour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Toasted Malted Wheat Flour is available in two main types in the market - Diastatic and Non-Diastatic. Diastatic Toasted Malted Wheat Flour contains active enzymes that break down starches into sugars, aiding in fermentation and providing a lighter texture to baked goods. On the other hand, Non-Diastatic Toasted Malted Wheat Flour has been heat-treated to deactivate these enzymes, making it ideal for adding color, flavor, and a slightly malty taste to baked goods without affecting fermentation. Both types offer unique characteristics for different culinary applications.</p></p>
