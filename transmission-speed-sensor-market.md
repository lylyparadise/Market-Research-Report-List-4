<p><ul><li>Input Speed</li><li>Output Speed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transmission speed sensor market consists of two main types: input speed sensors and output speed sensors. Input speed sensors measure the rotational speed of the input shaft in a transmission system, providing crucial data for the vehicle's control systems. On the other hand, output speed sensors measure the rotational speed of the output shaft, which is essential for monitoring the performance of the transmission and ensuring smooth operation of the vehicle. Both types of sensors play a vital role in optimizing the efficiency and reliability of transmission systems in various industries.</p></p>
