<p><p>Steel cord market types are classified based on their tensile strength: Normal Tensile (NT), High Tensile (HT), Super Tensile (ST), and Ultra Tensile (UT). NT cords have standard strength, suitable for general applications. HT cords offer increased strength, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. ST cords provide even higher tensile strength for specialized uses. UT cords feature the highest strength level, designed for extreme conditions where maximum durability and performance are required. Each type caters to specific needs in various industries.</p></p>
