<p><p>Oil and Gas Pipeline Leak Detection Market Types include Mass-Volume Balance, Acoustic/Ultrasonic, Fiber Optics, and Vapor Sensing technologies. Mass-Volume Balance method uses flow metering to detect discrepancies indicating a leak. Acoustic/Ultrasonic technology detects leaks by listening for changes in sound frequencies. Fiber Optics technology uses fiber optic cables to detect changes in temperature and strain caused by leaks. Vapor Sensing technology detects leaks by analyzing chemical changes in the air surrounding the pipeline.</p></p>
