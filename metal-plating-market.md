<p><p>Metal plating market consists of two main types: Electroplating and Electroless Plating. Electroplating involves the use of an electric current to deposit metal ions onto a substrate, creating a thin layer of metal coating. On the other hand, Electroless Plating is a chemical process that does not require an external power source, relying instead on a chemical reaction to deposit the metal coating onto the substrate. Both methods are widely used in various industries for improving the appearance and properties of metal surfaces.</p></p>
