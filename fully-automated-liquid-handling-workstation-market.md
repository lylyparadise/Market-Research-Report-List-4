<p><p>Fully Automated Liquid Handling Workstations are essential tools in research and clinical laboratories for precise and efficient liquid handling tasks. Two common types of liquid handling workstations are Non-contact and Contact type. Non-contact liquid handling workstations use air displacement technology to accurately dispense liquids without physically touching the sample. Contact type liquid handling workstations, on the other hand, use physical contact with the sample for precise dispensing. Both types offer high throughput and reproducibility, catering to different needs in the laboratory setting.</p></p>
