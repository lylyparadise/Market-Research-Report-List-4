Electrosurgical generators provide the necessary power and control the electrical current. Return electrodes disperse the current safely back to the generator. Accessories such as cables and electrodes support the functioning of the overall system. These components collectively enable precise and efficient tissue cutting and coagulation during surgical procedures.</p></p>
