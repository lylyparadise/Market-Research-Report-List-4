<p><ul><li>Fully Automatic</li><li>Semi-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fully automatic wafer vacuum mounters are designed to cater to high-volume production lines by offering fast and efficient mounting of wafers onto substrates. These machines require minimal human intervention and are equipped with advanced features such as automatic loading and unloading of wafers, precise alignment systems, and comprehensive error detection mechanisms. On the other hand, semi-automatic wafer vacuum mounters are more suitable for smaller-scale production lines where some manual labor is acceptable. These machines offer a balance between automation and manual operation, allowing for flexibility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
