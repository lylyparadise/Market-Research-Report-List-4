<p><p>Sludge treatment systems are divided into several types, including Bubbling Fluidized Bed (BFB) Incineration System, Circulating Fluidized Bed (CFB) Incineration System, Biomass-Coal System, Vacuum Dryer, and Others. BFB and CFB systems involve burning sludge in a fluidized bed, while the biomass-coal system combines sludge with coal for incineration. Vacuum dryers remove moisture from sludge to reduce volume. Other systems may include advanced technologies such as chemical treatment or thermal hydrolysis to treat sludge effectively.</p></p>
