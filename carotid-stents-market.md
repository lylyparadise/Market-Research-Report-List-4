<p><p>Carotid stents market includes two main types: Balloon Expandable Stents and Self-Expanding Stents. Balloon Expandable Stents are inserted into the carotid artery and then expanded using a balloon to support the artery walls and improve blood flow. On the other hand, Self-Expanding Stents are designed to automatically expand once inserted into the artery, providing a stable structure to keep the artery open.Both types of stents are used in the treatment of carotid artery disease to prevent strokes and other cardiovascular issues.</p></p>
