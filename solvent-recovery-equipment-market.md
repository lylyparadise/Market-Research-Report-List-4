<p><p>Solvent recovery equipment is divided into two main types: On-site solvent recovery equipment and Off-site solvent recovery equipment. On-site solvent recovery equipment is installed and operated directly at the facility where the solvent is used, allowing for immediate reclamation and reuse of solvent within the same location. Off-site solvent recovery equipment, on the other hand, involves transporting used solvents to a separate facility for recovery and reuse. Both types of equipment are essential in reducing solvent waste, increasing cost efficiency, and promoting environmental sustainability.</p></p>
