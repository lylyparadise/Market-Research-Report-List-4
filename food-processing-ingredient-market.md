<p><p>Food processing ingredient market types include modified starch & starch derivatives, protein, food stabilizers, emulsifiers, yeast, enzymes, acidity regulators, antioxidants, and release agents. Modified starch and starch derivatives are used as thickeners and stabilizers, while proteins provide nutritional benefits. Food stabilizers help maintain consistency and texture in products, and emulsifiers aid in mixing oil and water-based ingredients. Yeast is used in baking for fermentation, enzymes help in breaking down certain components, acidity regulators maintain pH levels, antioxidants extend shelf life, and release agents prevent sticking during processing.</p></p>
