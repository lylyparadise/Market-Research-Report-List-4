<p><p>Neck orthosis systems can be categorized into two main types: inlay type and externally assisted type. Inlay type neck orthosis systems are devices that are inserted directly onto the neck to provide support and stabilization. Externally assisted neck orthosis systems, on the other hand, are devices that are worn around the neck and are equipped with adjustable straps or mechanisms to provide external support. Both types of neck orthosis systems serve the purpose of immobilizing and protecting the neck in cases of injury or medical conditions.</p></p>
