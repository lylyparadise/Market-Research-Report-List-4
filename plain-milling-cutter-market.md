<p><ul><li>Disc Shape</li><li>Cylindrical Shape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plain milling cutters are commonly categorized into two main types based on their shape: disc shape and cylindrical shape.</p><p>Disc shape milling cutters are characterized by their flat circular shape, typically used for creating flat surfaces or grooves on a workpiece. They are versatile and precise, making them ideal for a wide range of milling applications.</p><p>Cylindrical shape milling cutters, on the other hand, have a cylindrical body and are commonly used for creating slots, pockets, and other complex geometries on a workpiece. They are known for their durability and efficiency in removing material.</p></p>
