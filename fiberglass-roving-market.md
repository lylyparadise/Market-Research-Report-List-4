<p><strong>The Fiberglass Roving Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-end Roving</li><li>Multi-end Roving</li><li>Chopped Roving</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiberglass roving is available in different forms to cater to various market needs. Single-end roving consists of continuous glass filaments wound into a single bundle, suitable for filament winding and pultrusion processes. Multi-end roving comprises multiple strands of glass filaments twisted together, ideal for spray-up and continuous roving processes. Chopped roving is chopped into short lengths and used for reinforcing thermoplastics and injection molding. Each type of roving serves specific applications in industries such as construction, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
