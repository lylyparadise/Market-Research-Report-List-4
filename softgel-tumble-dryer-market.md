<p><ul><li>Standard Tumble Drying</li><li>Continuous Tumble Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Standard Tumble Drying refers to the traditional method of drying softgel capsules in a rotating drum with hot air. This process is typically used for smaller batch sizes and is more time-consuming. On the other hand, Continuous Tumble Drying involves a continuous flow of softgel capsules through a drying chamber, resulting in a more efficient and faster drying process suitable for larger production volumes. Both types of tumble drying methods are commonly used in the softgel manufacturing industry to ensure the capsules are properly dried before packaging.</p></p>
