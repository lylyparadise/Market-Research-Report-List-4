<p><p>PE Foam market is divided into three types: IXPE, XPE, and EPE. XPE foam is known for its durability and shock absorption capabilities, commonly used in packaging and sporting goods. EPE foam, on the other hand, is lightweight and flexible, making it suitable for cushioning and protective packaging. Each type of PE foam offers unique properties catering to different market needs.</p></p>
