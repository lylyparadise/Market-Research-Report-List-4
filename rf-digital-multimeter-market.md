<p><p>RF Digital Multimeter refers to electronic measurement instruments used to measure parameters like voltage, current, and resistance in RF circuits. These meters come in three main types: Benchtop, Portable, and Modular. Benchtop models are larger and designed for stationary use in laboratories or workshops. Portable meters are compact and battery-operated, suitable for on-the-go measurements. Modular meters offer customizable configurations with interchangeable modules to suit specific testing requirements. Each type serves different purposes based on their size, portability, and versatility.</p></p>
