<p><p>Miniload storage systems are classified into Single Shuttle System and Dual Shuttle System markets based on their design and capabilities. The Single Shuttle System features one shuttle that moves within the storage system to retrieve items, offering efficient and precise movements for smaller warehouses or distribution centers. On the other hand, the Dual Shuttle System comprises two shuttles working simultaneously to increase productivity and speed, making it ideal for larger warehouses with high storage and retrieval demands.</p></p>
