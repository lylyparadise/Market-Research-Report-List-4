<p><strong>The Carrier Ethernet Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EPL Services</li><li>EVPL Services</li><li>E-LAN Services</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carrier Ethernet Services can be categorized into three main types: EPL Services, EVPL Services, and E-LAN Services. EPL Services are point-to-point Ethernet private line services suitable for connecting two locations. EVPL Services, on the other hand, are multipoint-to-multipoint Ethernet virtual private line services that allow for multiple locations to be connected. Lastly, E-LAN Services are multipoint-to-multipoint Ethernet LAN services that provide a fully meshed network for multiple locations to communicate with each other seamlessly.</p></p>
