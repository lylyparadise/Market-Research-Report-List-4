<p><strong>The Digital Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Water-based</li><li>UV-cured</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital ink markets can be categorized into four types based on their composition and properties. Solvent-based inks use organic solvents to dissolve the pigments and binders. Water-based inks use water as the main solvent, making them more environmentally friendly. UV-cured inks are cured instantly using ultraviolet light, resulting in high durability and scratch resistance. Other types of inks may include latex-based, eco-solvent, or dye-sublimation inks, each offering unique benefits for different printing applications. Each type of ink has its own advantages and limitations in terms of color accuracy, durability, and environmental impact.</p></p>
