Class 2 forklift trucks consist of electric motor narrow aisle trucks for warehouses. Class 3 forklift trucks are electric motor hand or hand-rider trucks for pallet handling. Class 4/5 forklift trucks are internal combustion engine trucks for heavy-duty outdoor use. Each class serves specific purposes in various industries such as logistics, manufacturing, and construction.</p></p>
