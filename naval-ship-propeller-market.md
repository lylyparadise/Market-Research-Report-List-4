<p><p>The naval ship propeller market can be categorized into three main types: Fixed Pitch Propeller, Controllable Pitch Propeller, and Others. Fixed Pitch Propellers have a fixed blade angle and are less complex but offer limited maneuverability. Controllable Pitch Propellers allow for adjusting the blade angle to optimize efficiency and performance. The "Others" category includes specialized propeller designs such as ducted propellers or hybrid propulsion systems. Each type has its own advantages and is used based on the specific requirements of the naval vessel.</p></p>
