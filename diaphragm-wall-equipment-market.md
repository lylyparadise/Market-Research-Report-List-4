<p><p>Diaphragm wall equipment market can be categorized into two main types - hydraulic diaphragm wall equipment and non-hydraulic diaphragm wall equipment. Hydraulic diaphragm wall equipment utilizes hydraulic power to create the diaphragm walls, offering higher precision and efficiency in construction. On the other hand, non-hydraulic diaphragm wall equipment relies on other power sources such as mechanical or electric to perform the same function. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in the construction industry for creating strong and durable underground walls.</p></p>
