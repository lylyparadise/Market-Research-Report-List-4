<p><p>The glass tubes market can be categorized into three main types: flint glass, borosilicate glass, and others. Flint glass is known for its optical clarity and high refractive index, making it ideal for applications like lenses and prisms. Borosilicate glass, on the other hand, is highly resistant to thermal shock and is commonly used in laboratory glassware and industrial applications. The "others" category includes specialty glass types designed for specific applications, such as soda-lime glass and lead glass.</p></p>
