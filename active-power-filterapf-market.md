<p><strong>The Active Power Filter(APF) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Active Power Filter</li><li>Series Active Power Filter</li><li>Hybrid Active Power Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active Power Filters (APF) are devices used to eliminate harmonics and correct power factor in electrical systems. There are three main types of APF: Shunt APF is connected in parallel with the load to mitigate harmonic currents, Series APF is placed in series with the load to compensate for harmonic voltages, and Hybrid APF combines both shunt and series configurations for effective harmonic filtering. Each type of APF serves a different purpose in improving power quality and reducing energy losses in electrical systems.</p></p>
