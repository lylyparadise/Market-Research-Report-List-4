<p><p>Green walls are vertical gardens that can be categorized into two main types based on the medium used for plant growth: soil-less and soil-based. Soil-less green walls utilize alternative growing mediums such as felt pockets or hydroponic systems to support plant life without traditional soil. Soil-based green walls, on the other hand, rely on soil as the primary medium for plant growth. Both types offer a sustainable and aesthetically pleasing solution for incorporating greenery into urban spaces.</p></p>
