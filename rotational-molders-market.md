<p><p>Rotational molding, also known as rotomolding, is a manufacturing process used to create hollow plastic parts. Rotational molders use a heated mold that rotates on two axes to evenly coat the interior of the mold with melted plastic material. This process is commonly used to create large, low-volume products such as storage tanks, toys, and automotive parts.</p><p>The Rotational Molders Market is currently experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 9.5% during the forecast period.
