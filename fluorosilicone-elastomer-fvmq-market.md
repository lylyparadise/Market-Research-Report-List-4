<p><p>Fluorosilicone Elastomer (FVMQ) is available in three primary types: Liquid FVMQ, Solid FVMQ, and Semisolid FVMQ. Liquid FVMQ is a popular choice for applications requiring low viscosity and easy processability. Solid FVMQ, on the other hand, is preferred for applications needing high temperature resistance and mechanical properties. Semisolid FVMQ offers a middle ground between the two, providing a balance of viscosity and mechanical properties. Each type of FVMQ caters to different requirements in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
