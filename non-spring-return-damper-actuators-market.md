<p><p>Non-Spring Return Damper Actuators come in various types based on their control mechanisms. Two-Position Control actuators have two distinct positions, fully open and fully closed. Three-Position Control actuators have three positions: fully open, fully closed, and a mid-position for partial opening or closing. Other types include modulating actuators which allow for variable positioning in between fully open and fully closed. These different types cater to various requirements in HVAC systems, providing flexibility in control and optimization of airflow.</p></p>
