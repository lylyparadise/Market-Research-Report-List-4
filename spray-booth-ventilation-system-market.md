<p><strong>The Spray Booth Ventilation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Side Draft Spray Booth Ventilation System</li><li>Cross Draft Spray Booth Ventilation System</li><li>Down Draft Spray Booth Ventilation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spray booth ventilation systems play a crucial role in maintaining a safe working environment for painting and coating applications. The three main types are side draft, cross draft, and down draft systems. Side draft systems pull air through the booth horizontally, cross draft systems use a horizontal flow of air across the workpiece, and down draft systems draw air vertically through the booth floor. Each system has its own advantages and is suited for different types of applications and work environments.</p></p>
