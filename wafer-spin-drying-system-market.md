<p><p>Single Stack System typically consists of one dryer chamber where wafers are spun to remove excess water or chemicals after washing. This system is often used for smaller production volumes or in research settings. Dual Stack System, on the other hand, includes two dryer chambers stacked on top of each other, allowing for higher throughput and efficiency. This system is commonly utilized in larger manufacturing facilities where a higher volume of wafers need to be processed quickly.</p></p>
