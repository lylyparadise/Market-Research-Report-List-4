<p><p>Laser Glass Processing Systems are primarily categorized into three types based on the laser source they use - Ultra-short Pulse Source, CO2, and UV. Ultra-short Pulse Source systems are used for high precision and extremely fine processing. CO2 systems are commonly used for cutting, engraving, and marking applications due to their high power output. UV systems are utilized for applications requiring minimal heat affected zones and high accuracy. Each type of system offers specific advantages and is chosen based on the requirements of the processing task.</p></p>
