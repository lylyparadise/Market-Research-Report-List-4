<p><p>Connected Rail Solution includes three main market types: Active Train Control (PTC), Communication/Computer Based Train Control (CBTC), and Automatic/Integrated Train Control (ATC). PTC involves technologies such as GPS and radio communication to monitor and control trains in real-time. CBTC utilizes computers and communication systems to manage train movements efficiently. ATC integrates various control systems to automate train operation, improve safety, and enhance overall network performance. These solutions play a crucial role in modernizing and optimizing rail systems to meet growing transportation demands.</p></p>
