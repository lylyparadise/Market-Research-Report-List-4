<p><p>Polyacrylamide market is categorized into Non-Ionic Polyacrylamide (PAMN), Anionic Polyacrylamide (APAM), Cationic Polyacrylamide (CPAM), and Others. Non-Ionic Polyacrylamide (PAMN) does not carry any charge and is used for various industrial applications. Anionic Polyacrylamide (APAM) carries a negative charge and is primarily used in wastewater treatment. Cationic Polyacrylamide (CPAM) carries a positive charge and is often used in sludge dewatering processes. Other types of polyacrylamides include a combination of ionic and non-ionic properties catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>
