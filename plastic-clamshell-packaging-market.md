<p><p>Plastic clamshell packaging is categorized into different market types based on the material used. PVC (Polyvinyl chloride) clamshell packaging is known for its durability and flexibility. PET (Polyethylene terephthalate) clamshells are lightweight and have excellent clarity. Polystyrene clamshell packaging is cost-effective and commonly used for food packaging. ABS (Acrylonitrile, Butadiene, and Styrene) clamshells are known for their impact resistance and heat resistance. Each of these materials offers specific advantages and is chosen based on the intended use of the packaging.</p></p>
