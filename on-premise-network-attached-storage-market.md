<p><p>On premise Network Attached Storage (NAS) refers to a network storage device that is connected to a local area network and provides data access to multiple clients. On premise NAS offers better security, control, and speed compared to cloud-based storage solutions, making it a preferred choice for many businesses.</p><p>The On premise NAS market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.50% during the forecast period. The increasing need for efficient data storage solutions, along with the rise in data volumes generated by organizations, is driving the growth of the market. Additionally, the growing adoption of advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, and IoT is further fueling the demand for on premise NAS solutions.</p><p>The latest trends in the On premise NAS market include the integration of NAS with cloud storage for hybrid deployment, the use of flash-based storage for faster data access, and the implementation of advanced security features to protect sensitive data.
