<p><p>Cystoscopy is a procedure used to examine the inside of the bladder and urethra. There are two main types of cystoscopes used for this procedure: rigid cystoscopes and flexible cystoscopes. Rigid cystoscopes are straight and stiff, allowing for a direct view of the bladder. Flexible cystoscopes, on the other hand, are more maneuverable and can navigate bends in the urethra for a more comprehensive examination. Both types of cystoscopes play a crucial role in diagnosing and treating various bladder and urinary tract conditions.</p></p>
