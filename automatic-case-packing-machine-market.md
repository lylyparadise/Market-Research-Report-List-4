<p><p>Automatic case packing machines are widely used in various industries for efficiently packing products into cases. There are two main types of these machines: Vertical Form Fill Seal (V-FFS) and Horizontal Form Fill Seal (H-FFS). V-FFS machines are vertical machines that form, fill, and seal the product in a pouch before packing it into cases. On the other hand, H-FFS machines are horizontal machines that form, fill, and seal the product in a case directly. Both types offer different advantages based on the specific needs of the industry.</p></p>
