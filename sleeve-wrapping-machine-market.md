<p><p>Sleeve wrapping machines come in two main market types: in-line feeding and side-feeding. In-line feeding machines are designed to wrap products that are fed into the machine in a straight line, making them ideal for high-speed packaging operations. On the other hand, side-feeding machines are suitable for wrapping products that are fed into the machine from the side, offering more flexibility in terms of the shape and size of the products being wrapped. Both types of machines are commonly used in industries such as food, beverage, and pharmaceuticals for packaging and sealing products efficiently.</p></p>
