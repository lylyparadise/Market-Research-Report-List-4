<p><p>Electric roll-up doors come in two main types - wall-mount rolling doors and floor-mount rolling doors. Wall-mount rolling doors are designed to be mounted to the wall, making them ideal for spaces with limited headroom. Floor-mount rolling doors are mounted directly to the floor, making them suitable for locations where wall mounting isn't possible. Both types of doors offer convenient and efficient access control, ensuring increased security and ease of use for various applications such as warehouses, garages, and commercial buildings.</p></p>
