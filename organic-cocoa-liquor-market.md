<p><p>Organic cocoa liquor markets can be categorized into two main types based on their physical forms: solid form and semi-solid form. Solid form organic cocoa liquor typically comes in the form of solid blocks or buttons that contain a high concentration of cocoa solids and cocoa butter. On the other hand, semi-solid form organic cocoa liquor has a softer texture and can be found in the form of cocoa paste or cocoa mass. Both types of organic cocoa liquor are used in various applications in the food and beverage industry, such as in the production of chocolate and confectionery products.</p></p>
