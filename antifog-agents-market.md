<p><p>Antifog agents are categorized into two main types: Short Term Type and Long Term Type. The Short Term Type antifog agents provide temporary protection against fogging for a brief period of time, typically lasting a few hours. On the other hand, Long Term Type antifog agents offer more lasting protection, with their effects lasting for an extended period, often weeks or even months. Both types of antifog agents are commonly used in various industries to prevent fogging on surfaces such as lenses, mirrors, and packaging material.</p></p>
