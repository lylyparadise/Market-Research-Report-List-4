<p><p>Epoxy resin films can be categorized into two main types: transparent and opaque. Transparent epoxy resin films allow light to pass through, giving a clear and glossy finish. These films are often used when a smooth and glossy surface is desired, as they enhance the appearance of the underlying material. Opaque epoxy resin films, on the other hand, do not allow light to pass through and provide a solid color or patterned finish. These films are commonly used for their ability to conceal imperfections or add decorative elements to surfaces.</p></p>
