<p><strong>The HIV Therapeutics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nucleoside-Analog Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors (NRTIs)</li><li>Non-Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors (NNRTIs)</li><li>Entry and Fusion Inhibitors</li><li>Protease Inhibitors (PIs)</li><li>Integrase Inhibitors</li><li>Coreceptor Antagonists</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HIV therapeutics market involves various types of medications to treat HIV infection. Nucleoside-Analog Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors (NRTIs) and Non-Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors (NNRTIs) target enzymes essential for HIV replication. Entry and Fusion Inhibitors prevent the virus from entering cells. Protease Inhibitors (PIs) block viral enzyme activity. Integrase Inhibitors prevent the integration of viral DNA into the host cell genome. Coreceptor Antagonists block viral entry by targeting specific receptors on immune cells. Each type of medication plays a crucial role in managing HIV infection.</p></p>
