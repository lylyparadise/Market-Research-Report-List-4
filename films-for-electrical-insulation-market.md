<p><p>Films for electrical insulation are used to prevent electrical conductors from coming into contact with each other and causing a short circuit. There are different types of films used for electrical insulation, including polyester, polypropylene, and other materials. Polyester films offer high thermal resistance and can withstand high temperatures. Polypropylene films are known for their excellent dielectric properties and are commonly used in capacitors. Other materials, such as PVC or Mylar, may also be used for specific applications in the electrical insulation market.</p></p>
