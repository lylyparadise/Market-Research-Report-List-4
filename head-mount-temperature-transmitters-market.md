<p><p>Head-Mount Temperature Transmitters are devices used to measure and transmit temperature data in various industrial processes. There are two main types of temperature sensors used in these transmitters: Thermocouples and Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs). Thermocouples measure temperature by detecting the voltage produced when two different metals are joined at one end, while RTDs measure temperature by changes in the electrical resistance of a metal wire. Both types are commonly used in industrial settings for their accuracy and reliability in temperature monitoring.</p></p>
