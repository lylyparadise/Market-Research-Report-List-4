<p><p>Optical biosensor market can be classified into two main types - type 1 and type 2. Type 1 includes biosensors that are based on the measurement of light intensity or wavelength changes caused by biomolecular interactions. These sensors are highly sensitive and are widely used in research laboratories. Type 2 biosensors, on the other hand, utilize the changes in the refractive index of a medium due to biomolecular interactions. These sensors are commonly used in medical diagnostics and environmental monitoring applications due to their high specificity and accuracy.</p></p>
