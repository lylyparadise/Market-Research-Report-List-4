<p><ul><li>Straight Guide Wire</li><li>Angled Guide Wire</li><li>J-Shape Guide Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Peripheral guidewires are medical devices used in various procedures to guide catheters through blood vessels. Straight guidewires provide easy navigation through vessels with minimal resistance. Angled guidewires have a bend at the tip that aids in steering through complex anatomy. J-shape guidewires are designed for more precise navigation and access to vessels in challenging areas. Each type of guidewire caters to different procedural requirements and patient conditions, offering healthcare professionals a range of options for effective and safe navigation during procedures.</p></p>
