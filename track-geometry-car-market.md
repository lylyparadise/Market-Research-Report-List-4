<p><p>Track geometry cars are used for inspecting and maintaining railroad tracks. There are two main types of markets for these cars: contact measurement and non-contact measurement. Contact measurement cars physically touch the track to collect data, while non-contact measurement cars use sensors and cameras to collect data without physically touching the track. Each market type offers unique advantages and disadvantages based on the specific needs of the railroad company and the type of track being inspected.</p></p>
