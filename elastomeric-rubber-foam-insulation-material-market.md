<p><p>Elastomeric rubber foam insulation materials come in various types including NBR based, EPDM based, chloroprene based, and others. NBR based insulation materials are known for their excellent oil resistance. EPDM based materials are resistant to weathering and UV exposure, making them suitable for outdoor applications. Chloroprene based materials offer good resistance to chemicals and ozone. Other types of elastomeric rubber foam insulation materials may include blends of different rubber compounds to provide specific properties tailored to different applications.</p></p>
