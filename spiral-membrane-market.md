<p><p>Spiral membrane technology includes four main market types: Reverse Osmosis (RO), Nanofiltration (NF), Ultrafiltration (UF), and Microfiltration (MF). MF membranes are used for removing bacteria and suspended solids. Each type of membrane offers unique filtration properties and is used in various industries for water treatment and purification purposes.</p></p>
