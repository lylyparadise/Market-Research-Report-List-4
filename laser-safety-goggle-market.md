<p><p>Laser safety goggles are divided into two main types: absorbent goggles and reflective goggles. Absorbent goggles are designed to absorb the laser light and prevent it from reaching the eyes, offering protection by absorbing the laser energy. Reflective goggles, on the other hand, reflect laser light away from the eyes, preventing it from causing harm. Both types of goggles are crucial in ensuring the safety of individuals working with lasers, with each type offering unique benefits depending on the specific application and wavelength of the laser.</p></p>
