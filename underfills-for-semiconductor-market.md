<p><p>Underfills for semiconductor market types include chip-on-film underfills, flip chip underfills, and CSP/BGA board level underfills. Chip-on-film underfills provide stress relief and protection for the chip attached to a flexible film. Flip chip underfills enhance the reliability of flip chip bonding by filling the space between the chip and substrate. CSP/BGA board level underfills improve the mechanical strength and reliability of solder joints in high-density packaging applications. These underfills play a crucial role in ensuring the performance and longevity of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
