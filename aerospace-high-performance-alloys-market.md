Cobalt-based alloys offer excellent corrosion resistance and high strength, making them suitable for use in turbine engines and other aerospace components. Nickel-based alloys are widely used in the aerospace industry due to their exceptional mechanical properties, thermal stability, and resistance to corrosion and oxidation.</p></p>
