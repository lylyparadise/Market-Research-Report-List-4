<p><strong>The Two Shot Injection Molding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multiple Injection Molding</li><li>Embedded Injection Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multiple Injection Molding involves injecting two different materials into a mold simultaneously to create a single part with two different colors or materials. This technique is commonly used in the production of consumer products and automotive components. On the other hand, Embedded Injection Molding is a process where one material is injected into another, creating a part with embedded features. This method is often utilized in the manufacturing of electronic components and medical devices. Both types of injection molding offer unique advantages in terms of design flexibility and cost-efficiency.</p></p>
