<p><p>A railway pantograph is a device mounted on the roof of an electric train that collects electricity from overhead lines to power the train's engine. It consists of a metal arm equipped with carbon strips that come into contact with the overhead wires, allowing the electricity to flow into the train and power its operations. Pantographs are crucial components in electric trains as they enable efficient and reliable distribution of power from the overhead wires to the train.</p><p>The Railway Pantograph Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.8% during the forecast period.
