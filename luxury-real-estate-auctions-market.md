<p><p>Luxury real estate auctions can be categorized into two main market types: Reserved Auction and Unreserved Auction. In a Reserved Auction, the seller sets a minimum price (reserve price) that must be met in order for the property to be sold. On the other hand, in an Unreserved Auction, there is no minimum price set, and the property will be sold to the highest bidder regardless of the final price. These market types offer different levels of risk and reward for both the seller and the buyer.</p></p>
