<p><p>Electric tube furnaces can be categorized into two main types based on their orientation: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal tube furnaces are ideal for applications that require uniform heating of long samples or materials. They are commonly used in industries such as metallurgy, ceramics, and semiconductor processing. On the other hand, vertical tube furnaces are suitable for applications that involve vertical sample insertion and extraction. They are commonly used in research laboratories for processes such as chemical vapor deposition and thermal treatments.</p></p>
