<p><ul><li>Capillary Flow Underfill</li><li>No Flow Underfill</li><li>Molded Underfill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capillary Flow Underfill dispensers utilize capillary action to fill gaps between components and substrates, providing a strong bond. No Flow Underfill dispensers are designed for applications where flow control is critical, preventing underfill from spreading to unwanted areas. Molded Underfill dispensers are used for encapsulating components in molded packages, providing protection and increasing mechanical strength. Each type of underfill dispenser caters to specific needs and requirements in the electronics industry, ensuring reliable and efficient bonding for various applications.</p></p>
