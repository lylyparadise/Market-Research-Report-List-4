<p><ul><li>Steam Dried(SD) Fish Powder</li><li>Flame Dried(FD) Fish Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fish powder market can be categorized into two main types - Steam Dried (SD) Fish Powder and Flame Dried (FD) Fish Powder. </p><p>Steam Dried Fish Powder is made by cooking fresh fish in steam and then drying and grinding it into a powder. This process helps in retaining the natural flavor and nutrients of the fish. On the other hand, Flame Dried Fish Powder is made by cooking fresh fish over a flame and then drying and grinding it into a powder. This method gives the fish powder a unique flavor and aroma.</p></p>
