Two common types of aeration systems are disc aeration systems and pipe aeration systems. Disc aeration systems use rotating discs to agitate and aerate the water, while pipe aeration systems involve pumping air through pipes to create bubbles that oxygenate the water. Both types of systems are crucial in promoting efficient and effective wastewater treatment processes.</p></p>
