<p><p>Fluoropolymers market consists of different types such as Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF), Fluoroelastomers, and other specialty fluoropolymers. PTFE is known for its non-stick properties and chemical resistance, PVDF is valued for its high corrosion resistance, Fluoroelastomers are utilized for their flexibility and heat resistance, and other specialty fluoropolymers offer unique properties for specific applications. Each type has its own distinct features and uses in various industries such as automotive, electronics, and construction.</p></p>
