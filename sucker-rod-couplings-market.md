<p><p>Class T and Class SM sucker rod couplings are two types of sucker rod couplings commonly used in the oil and gas industry. Class T couplings are designed for moderate to heavy loads, while Class SM couplings are specifically for severe loading conditions. Class T couplings typically have thicker walls and larger threads compared to Class SM couplings. Both types play a crucial role in connecting sucker rods, ensuring efficient and reliable operation of pumping systems in oil wells.</p></p>
