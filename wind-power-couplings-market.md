<p><p>Wind power couplings are typically classified into two main types: rigid and flexible. Rigid couplings offer high torsional stiffness and are ideal for applications where precise alignment is crucial. On the other hand, flexible couplings are designed to absorb shocks and vibrations, providing smoother operation and reducing wear on machinery. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the wind power system. The market for these couplings is diverse, catering to a wide range of applications in the wind power industry.</p></p>
