<p><ul><li>Front Opening Shipping Box (FOSB)</li><li>Front Opening Unified Pod (FOUP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wafer carrier boxes are used in the semiconductor industry to transport wafers safely. Two common types are Front Opening Shipping Box (FOSB) and Front Opening Unified Pod (FOUP). The FOSB boxes have a front opening for easy loading and unloading of wafers, making them ideal for shipping. On the other hand, FOUP boxes are used for storing wafers in a clean and controlled environment, ensuring their quality and integrity. Both types play a crucial role in the semiconductor manufacturing process.</p></p>
