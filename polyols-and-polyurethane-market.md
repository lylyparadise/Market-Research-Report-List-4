<p><p>Polyols are chemical compounds used in the production of polyurethane, a versatile material used in various industries. There are two main types of polyols: Polyether Polyols and Polyester Polyols. Polyether Polyols are made by reacting ethylene or propylene oxide with a compound containing active hydrogen atoms, while Polyester Polyols are produced by reacting diols with dicarboxylic acids. Both types of polyols play a crucial role in the formulation of polyurethane, offering different properties and applications based on their chemical structure.</p></p>
