<p><p>Rolling stock wire harness market can be categorized based on the voltage levels they are designed to handle. Low voltage wire harnesses are used for applications requiring minimal power, such as lighting and control systems. Medium voltage wire harnesses are used for power transmission and distribution, typically in locomotives and passenger cars. High voltage wire harnesses are designed for heavy-duty applications such as traction motors and power converters. Each type of wire harness is specific to the voltage requirements of the rolling stock it is installed in.</p></p>
