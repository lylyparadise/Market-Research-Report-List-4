<p><p>Glass block markets are divided into two main types based on how the glass is treated: tempered and annealed. Tempered glass blocks are heat-treated to increase strength and safety, making them resistant to breakage and better for high-impact areas. Annealed glass blocks, on the other hand, are not heat-treated and are more prone to breaking but are easier to cut and shape. Both types offer different benefits and are used in various applications depending on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
