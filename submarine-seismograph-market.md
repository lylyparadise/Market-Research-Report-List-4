<p><strong>The Submarine Seismograph Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tethered</li><li>Self-floating</li><li>Cable</li><li>Sputnik</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Submarine seismographs come in various types including tethered, self-floating, cable, and Sputnik. Tethered seismographs are connected to a ship by a cable for data transmission. Self-floating seismographs are designed to float freely in the water to collect seismic data. Cable seismographs are fixed to the seabed using a cable for stability. Sputnik seismographs are equipped with a buoyant device that rises to the surface after data collection. Each type of seismograph offers unique benefits and functionalities for underwater seismic monitoring.</p></p>
